Mr. Carlton Harris

ASSISTANT PRINCIPAL

Assistant Principal Carlton Harris joined the staff at Johns Creek in 2011. He is a native of Tampa, Florida and attended Hillsborough High School. Mr. Harris majored in Political Science and Mathematics from Florida A&M University. In addition to playing football, he was active in ROTC and was commissioned as a 2nd Lieutenant. He served in the United States Army for 5 years-active and reserve. He received his Master’s in Education from The University of West Georgia and Educational Specialist degree from Cambridge College. He has been in education for 25 years. He began his career at Paul D. W. Middle School where he was awarded Teacher of the Year. He served as an administrator at Creekside High School before coming to Johns Creek.

Mr. Jason Holcombe

ASSISTANT PRINCIPAL

Jason Holcombe joined the Gladiator family in 2015 as the Athletic Director. Coach Holcombe is a graduate of Brookwood High School in Snellville, Georgia. He graduated from the University of Georgia with a degree in Broad Field Social Science Education. He later received his Master’s in Educational Technology from Walden University, and earned his Specialist Degree from Lincoln Memorial University in Curriculum & Instruction. He served as teacher, coach and assistant Athletic Director at Dacula High School, and North Gwinnett High School, before serving as Assistant Principal and Athletic Director at Jackson County High School. Coach Holcombe came to Johns Creek in 2015 as the Athletic Director and Assistant Administrator. Coach Holcombe and his wife Amy have 3 children; Jason Jr., Georgia Nicole, & Harper Savannah.

Ms. Caren Hudson

ASSISTANT PRINCIPAL

Ms. Hudson is originally from Cornelia, Ga(Habersham County for those who know our far away opposing team to our north). She is a graduate of the University of Georgia where she got her Undergraduate and her Masters. She also worked for UGA as a student teacher coordinator while earning her Masters. She earned her leadership from Kennesaw State. Since she came to Fulton County in 1994 she has been at: Chattahoochee High School for 13 years as a department chair and 4 time State Winning Cheerleading Coach, Alpharetta High School for 4 years as an Instructional Support Specialist, opened Johns Creek High School in 2009 as Assistant Principal coached the 1st State Championship team for JCHS for Cheerleading.

Mr. Patrick Martin

ASSISTANT PRINCIPAL

Mr. Martin grew up in upstate South Carolina, attended the University of Alabama (Roll Tide!), where he earned his Bachelors in Music Education. He also holds a Masters in Music Education degree from the University of Illinois, and leadership certification and a Specialist degree in Educational Administration and Policy from the University of Georgia. Mr. Martin joined JCHS as assistant principal in the fall of 2009, following a three-year stint as Student Support Coordinator at Northview High School. Before moving into administration, Mr. Martin taught instrumental music for 10 years in DeKalb and Fulton Counties. He lives in Johns Creek with his wife and daughter, who will eventually attend Johns Creek High School.

Mr. Mathew Vance

ASSITANT PRINCIPAL

Matt Vance is a veteran school administrator. Since he joined the Johns Creek administrative team, Vance has worked with the Science and World Language Departments as well as directing the JCHS Personalized Learning initiative. Vance also developed and implemented many programs to ensure student health and wellness, including a mentoring program focused on identifying and leveraging students’ gifts, abilities, and talents. He has presented to state, national, and international audiences on STEM instruction, Professional Learning Communities, and student mentoring. In 2014, Vance was recognized as the Fulton County Assistant Principal of the Year. He got his bachelor’s degree in Education at Oglethorpe University and received master’s and specialist degrees from Georgia State University in Education Leadership. He lives in Brookhaven with his wife Candice, daughter Sullivan Mackenzie, and two dogs Halley and Comet.

Dr. Jimmy Zoll

PRINCIPAL

Jimmy Zoll was born in Jacksonville, Florida in 1961 and moved to Atlanta in the same year. He graduated from North Fulton High School in 1979 and attended Young Harris College where he earned his Associate of Arts degree. From there, he went to the University of Georgia (go dawgs!) and graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Education. Jimmy received his Master of Education at Georgia State University and a Specialist in Education: Administration and Supervision Degree from the State University of West Georgia. Jimmy has been an assistant principal at College Park Elementary, and Brookview Elementary. In April of 2000, Jimmy became Principal of State Bridge Crossing Elementary. Jimmy became the principal of Autrey Mill Middle in 2011. During this time, Jimmy completed his PhD at Mercer University in Atlanta. In 2015, Jimmy was asked to become the principal of Johns Creek High School. He is married to Laura, Principal at Dolvin Elementary. His sons Andrew, a graduate of UGA and Christopher, a graduate of Georgia Southern, attended Fulton County Schools. They also have a dog, named Savannah, which they love to travel with to the beach.

  • Email: Zoll@fultonschools.org
