GRADUATION INFORMATION

FOR ALL SENIORS AND PARENTS



GRADUATION

MAY 26, 2016 AT 7PM - LAST DAY OF SCHOOL

Where: The graduation will be viewed from two spaces inside North Point Community Church. In addition to the East Auditorium where the ceremony will take place, there is a lovely Theater for overflow seating in close proximity to the East Auditorium. New this year, the graduates will process through both the Auditorium and the Theater at the beginning of the ceremony in order for all family members to be a part of the live ceremony. Both rooms will have theater-sized live video of the event, from pre-ceremony through the recessional and both rooms open into the same lobby area for mingling immediately after graduation.

Tickets: Tickets are required for entry to both the East Auditorium and the Theater. All graduates automatically receive 4 tickets for the East Auditorium seating. Due to limited seating, we are unable to accommodate requests for additional tickets for the East Auditorium. If you need additional tickets (for use in the Theater only) you may receive up to 4 tickets for the Theater in addition to your 4 Auditorium seats. Students will receive the four tickets to the East Auditorium at graduation practice on Wednesday, May 25th. (More information regarding graduation practice will be forthcoming.) For your additional guests who will be sitting in the Theater, tickets (up to four per family) will be available for pick up at the JCHS front office between Monday, March 21st and Thursday, May 26th.

Please email Patrick Martin, Assistant Principal at MartinP1@fultonschools.org if you have any questions regarding tickets for graduation.

All seniors will receive much more detailed communication and information regarding graduation ceremonies in the future.
