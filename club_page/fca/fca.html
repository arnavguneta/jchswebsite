About FCA

Mission Statement:"FCA focuses on serving Johns Creek by equipping, empowering, and encouraging people to make a difference for Christ."

What do We Do?
Every Friday morning, FCA meets for food, games, worship, and fellowship. A student or religious community leader then delivers a message. Outside of Friday mornings, FCA serves our school's athletic teams and our community.
